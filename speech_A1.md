# Speech Transcription — CEFR A1 (Beginner)

> Simple sentences, basic vocabulary, present tense, short phrases.

---

## Slide 1: The Breach (Lock being hacked)

"Look. This is a computer. Someone is trying to open it. They try many passwords. Wrong. Wrong. Wrong. Then... yes! They are inside. This person is a hacker."

---

## Slide 2: The Money (Scale balance)

"A hacker earns 150,000 dollars. But a company loses 4 million dollars without a hacker. Look at the balance. The hacker is cheap. The problem is expensive. Companies save 30 times more."

---

## Slide 3: The Pentagon (USA fortress breached)

"This is the Pentagon. It is in the USA. It is very important. The government said: 'Please, hack us.' In 13 minutes, someone found a problem. They found 138 problems!"

---

## Slide 4: How They Work (Live terminal)

"This is how a hacker works. First, they scan the computer. They find open doors. Then they go inside. They find the problem. Finally, they write a report. They send it to the company. Done."

---

## Slide 5: What Students Learn (Skill tree)

"What do you learn? Python, Linux, networking, cryptography, SQL injection, firewalls, WiFi hacking, and malware. You start with the basics. Then you go up. Like a video game."

---

## Slide 6: Choose Your Path

"Two paths. One is bad: prison, no internet, no future. The other is good: money, travel, freedom. Same skills. Different choice. Which one do you choose?"

---

## Presentation Tips

- Speak very slowly and clearly
- Point at the screen when numbers appear
- Use simple hand gestures
- Pause between sentences
- Smile at the audience
